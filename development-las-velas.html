About This Development

Penthouse Las Velas is an exclusive collection of 24 penthouses in the heart of Santa Pola, one of the Costa Blanca's most beloved coastal towns. Each home is designed to maximise the Mediterranean lifestyle with expansive rooftop terraces, open-plan living, and breathtaking sea views.

The architecture draws inspiration from traditional Mediterranean design — whitewashed volumes, natural stone accents, and large glass openings that dissolve the boundary between indoors and out. Interiors are finished to an impeccable standard: porcelain tile floors, fully equipped kitchens with premium appliances, and sleek contemporary bathrooms.

Every penthouse features a private rooftop terrace ranging from 30 to 60 m², perfect for alfresco dining, sunbathing, or simply watching the sunset over the Mediterranean. The top-floor units offer panoramic 360-degree views of the coastline and the nearby Santa Pola salt flats — a protected natural park.

The development includes landscaped communal areas, a rooftop infinity pool, and underground parking. Santa Pola's sandy beaches, marina, restaurants, and shops are all within a short walk. Alicante Airport is a convenient 15-minute drive.
