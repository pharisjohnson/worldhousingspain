About This Property

Exclusive Beachfront Apartment: Luxury, Design and Endless Mediterranean Views

Discover a true real estate masterpiece: a property that perfectly blends contemporary elegance, a prime beachfront location, and the finest high-end finishes. Built in 2019 and presented in impeccable condition, ready to move in, this spectacular residence is offered fully furnished with carefully selected wood and leather pieces that bring warmth, refinement, and understated sophistication to every space.

With 162 m² built area and 123 m² of living space, the apartment offers exceptional natural light, generous proportions, and a thoughtfully designed layout for effortless daily comfort.

Beachfront, Southeast facing — Wake up to unforgettable sunrises and enjoy magnificent natural light throughout the day. The expansive terrace overlooking the sea, clad in natural stone and equipped with electric awnings, offers uninterrupted panoramic views: the perfect setting to relax or entertain in style.

The kitchen has been enlarged from its original layout to create a more spacious and highly functional culinary space. It features high-end Siemens and Bosch appliances, exquisite Italian marble countertops, and elegant wood accents that perfectly combine durability with timeless design.

The property offers three spacious bedrooms and two full bathrooms, all with premium finishes and refined design details.

Premium finishes include: Porcelanosa porcelain tile flooring, central air conditioning and heating, electric windows with remote control, iron doors, reinforced main entrance door, mosquito nets throughout, recessed lighting, and electric awnings on the terrace.

The building features 24/7 security, alarm system, automatic electric entrance door, elevator, wide staircase, and a storage room on ground floor. A private parking space just 5 minutes away and an additional storage room are also included.

A unique opportunity for those seeking quality of life, exclusivity, and a prime beachfront location overlooking the Mediterranean. Ideal as a primary residence or a premium investment.
